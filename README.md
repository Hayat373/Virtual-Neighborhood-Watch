# Project Title: Virtual Neighborhood Watch

The Virtual Neighborhood Watch is a community-driven application designed to enhance safety and security in local neighborhoods. It empowers residents to actively participate in maintaining a safe environment by reporting suspicious activities, sharing safety tips, and staying informed about local incidents. By fostering communication and vigilance within the community, this platform aims to create a safer and more connected neighborhood.

## User Roles

### Admin
- Can view all incident reports and safety tips submitted by users.
- Has the ability to delete any reports or tips as needed.

### User
- Can report incidents and submit safety tips.
- Can only view and manage their own reports and tips.

## Features

### Incident Reporting (CRUD)

- **Create**: Users can report new suspicious activities in their area, detailing the nature of the incident and location.
  
- **Read**: Users can view all of their own reported incidents. Admins can view all reports submitted by users, allowing for comprehensive oversight.
  
- **Update**: Users have the ability to update their reports.
  
- **Delete**: Users can delete their own reports if they wish, maintaining control over their submissions. Admins can delete any report if necessary.

### Safety Tips Sharing (CRUD)

- **Create**: Users can submit safety tips that may help others in the community.
  
- **Read**: Users can browse all of their own submitted safety tips. Admins can view all tips submitted by users.
  
- **Update**: Users can edit their submitted tips to keep information relevant and accurate.
  
- **Delete**: Users can remove their tips if they no longer apply. Admins have the ability to delete any tip.
